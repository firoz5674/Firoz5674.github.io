who we are

Skyrise not just understands the need of the client but provides the best solution and services in the affordable range.

Skyrise Construction & Interiors company is founded by Mr. Mohd Rashid in 1998. We are involved in Construction and Interiors agency and serving our clients for more than 20 years in New Delhi. The company has achieved outstanding prominence in the journey of 20 years and this is an ongoing process as it leads to build a strong relationship with clients. The company involves and participates in the decision making process & gives expertise guidance as per clients requirement.

Skyrise Construction & Interiors is one of the leading constructor of Tensile Structures, Porta Cabin, PUF Insulated Panel, Aerocon Prefabricated Structure, Prefabricated Structure and specialized designers of residential and commercial interior.
